File:
	Save- This saves all the changes you made to your image!
	Save As- This lets you save over a different file, or make a new one so you don't replace your old image.
	Load- Use this to load a png, jpg, or bmp image into the editor!

Shapes:
	Size- This top option lets you change the size of your shapes! Goes between 1 and 50. Type in a value, or use the arrows.
	Line- Draws a line across the screen! Size controls the thickness!

Tool Color:
	Color Picker- You can pick the color of your pen and shapes here!
